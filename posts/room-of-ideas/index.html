A room for ideas

Turning the backlog into a landscape you can navigate.

processsystemswriting

Backlogs become weight when they are just lists. A landscape is different: it has paths, landmarks, and room to breathe. The trick is to give ideas a map.

Practice

Every week I rewrite the queue using a simple triage:

  • Fuel: concepts that energize the next cycle.
  • Signal: research that adds direction.
  • Archive: anything that needs rest.

When the queue becomes a map, it becomes usable again.
