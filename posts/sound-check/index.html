Sound check before launch

A short ritual for release days.

ritualsshippingmindset

Launch days are chaotic. A tiny ritual helps steady the tone.

The ritual

  1. Read the landing page aloud.
  2. Open the site on a phone you do not own.
  3. Ask, “What is still unclear?” and write down the first answer.

Why it works

Speaking the copy reveals friction. The borrowed phone reveals typography mistakes. The last question reveals if the story still lands.
